A Farewell From Executive Chairman DanDanTheCats (DDTC)

From 2022-2026, the RecTuberMultiShow meant more to me than I can properly put into words. What started as an idea turned into something real, something memorable, and something that brought people together in a way I will never forget.

When I first started the RecTuberMultiShow, I could never have fully imagined how much it would grow, how many people it would reach, and how many memories it would end up carrying. It became more than a project. It became a place where creators, friends, fans, and visitors could come together and be part of something special.

Over the years, the RecTuberMultiShow became a place full of moments I will always remember. There were shows, conversations, collaborations, surprises, and so many little things that slowly built into something bigger than I ever expected. Every person who joined, every person who supported it, and every person who believed in it helped shape what it became.

I want to thank everyone who supported this journey from the beginning and everyone who found it later and still gave it a chance. Thank you to the creators who brought energy, to the guests who brought personality, to the friends who made everything feel alive, and to the people who simply showed up and made the space feel real. A community is never built by one person alone. It is built by everyone who chooses to be part of it.

There were times when things were exciting, times when things were chaotic, and times when I had to push through challenges to keep moving forward. But even in the hard moments, I never forgot why I started. I wanted to make something meaningful. I wanted to build a place where Rec Room content could feel bigger, where people could connect, and where ideas could become something people actually remembered. Looking back now, I can honestly say that happened.

The RecTuberMultiShow was never just about a title or a logo. It was about the people who made it come alive. It was about creativity, friendship, hard work, and the feeling of seeing something you cared about grow into something real. It was about giving people a place to belong, a place to enjoy, and a place to make memories that felt worth keeping.

As this chapter comes to a close, I do not want this to feel like the end of something meaningless. I want it to feel like a proper farewell to something that truly mattered. Even if the page changes, even if the support form is gone, and even if the structure around it looks different, the memories are still there. The effort is still there. The community is still there in every moment that was shared.

To everyone who ever supported the RecTuberMultiShow in any way, thank you. Thank you for the visits, the kind words, the help, the patience, the excitement, the creativity, and the belief. Thank you for being part of something that meant a lot to me. That kind of support does not disappear. It stays with the project, and it stays with the person who built it.

I am proud of what the RecTuberMultiShow became from 2022-2026. I am proud of the memories it created. I am proud of the people it brought together. And I am proud that something that started as an idea turned into a real community space with its own story. That is not something I will ever take lightly.

So this is my farewell, not with regret, but with gratitude. Thank you for being part of the journey, thank you for helping make it what it was, and thank you for every moment that made the RecTuberMultiShow feel alive. From me, Executive Chairman DanDanTheCats (DDTC), thank you for everything.

With appreciation,
